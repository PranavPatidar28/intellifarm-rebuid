Single-Slide Pitch Version

From OTP login to smarter farm decisions

This version is optimized for a fast demo story: simple access, fast onboarding, one decision hub, and four high-value action loops that make Intellifarm feel complete in a hackathon pitch.

Low-friction access Phone-first login keeps the entry point understandable for judges and farmers.
3-step setup Profile, plot, and season convert a new user into an active farm workspace.
Action-oriented product loop Fields, planning, market insight, and support all feed back into weekly farm action.
